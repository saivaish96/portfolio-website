The Time Portal - An immersive experience

I always pictured a time machine in the form of a DeLorean, a spaceship-like vessel or perhaps even a red telephone booth. But, as it turns out, the time portal has now come in the form of a flying saucer. The Time Portal will be a roaming, immersive, interactive, science edutainment experience. Exploring our high-tech time portal, you’ll become a character in a sci-fi story with the power to travel through time and space.

About the Brand

The Time Portal is an immersive art and science virtual-reality (VR) space; designed to amaze, entertain, and inspire visitors of all ages.
