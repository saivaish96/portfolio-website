Hi I'm Sai Vaishnavi Swaminathan

I am a UX Designer & UX Engineer

"It was a pleasant evening after a good rainfall, I was outside playing, jumping across puddles when I witnessed a seven-colored spectrum of light and was fascinated to no end, by this splash of colors. This magical natural phenomenon has more to it than what meets the eye. I consider, my whimsical love for rainbows as the outset for my towering interest in user experience and design."

I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science from Anna University, Tamil Nadu, India. I always spent an insane amount of time coding and achieving output in black and white screens. However, I was over the moon seeing a colorful output when I developed a website for the first time. That was when I knew that I wanted a career in the UX field. Currently, I am pursuing my Masters in Human Computer Interaction at the University of Maryland, College Park.
